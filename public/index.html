Chattanooga Traditional Dance Society

We host regular contra dances on the 2nd & 4th Saturdays of each month. Beginner's lesson starts at 7:30 and the dance starts at 8:00. We dance in the gym of the East Ridge Presbyterian Church. The address is 4919 Court Dr. Chattanooga TN 37412.
